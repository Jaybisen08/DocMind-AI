Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about DocMind AI? Here are standard answers explaining how it works.

What file formats are supported?
DocMind AI supports standard formats including PDF, DOCX, and TXT files, making it easy to process textbooks, research papers, and company documents.
How does Gemini answer questions?
Google Gemini reads and analyzes the uploaded documents, extracting contextual information and combining it with state-of-the-art reasoning to provide highly accurate, contextual answers.
Is my document secure?
Yes, your documents are processed securely. Your data is protected, never used to train public models, and is accessible only within your authenticated session.
Can I download summaries?
Absolutely! Once Gemini generates a comprehensive summary, you can instantly export and download it as a professionally formatted PDF report.
Does it work for research papers?
Yes, DocMind AI is highly optimized for complex texts, technical whitepapers, and academic research papers, easily breaking down advanced jargon.
Can I generate quizzes from any PDF?
Yes! You can generate interactive quizzes from any uploaded document. The AI analyzes the text, generates relevant multiple-choice questions, and scores your answers instantly.
